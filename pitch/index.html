Technology Deep-Dive

Proprietary stack: hardware + adaptive optics + ML + software
FEMTOSECOND LASER WRITING

Ultrashort pulses (<300 fs) enable non-thermal, 3D material modification in glass, polymer and crystal — no masking or etching required.

ADAPTIVE OPTICS

HoloView wavefront sensor corrects aberrations in real time, enabling diffraction-limited focus deep inside substrates.

AI PROCESS CONTROL

ML models predict and compensate for material variation. Closed-loop feedback ensures ≥99% yield at production scale.
